About

Who We Are

JYA Inc. is a Plattsmouth, Nebraska–based consulting firm led by John Yonemori-Antal, PE, PMP. We deliver mission-critical project controls, cost engineering, and BIM/VDC strategy for federal agencies, defense programs, and general contractors operating in high-assurance environments.

With a global footprint spanning CONUS and OCONUS operations, JYA Inc. brings disciplined program oversight to Tier IV data centers, secure underground facilities, detention infrastructure, aviation programs, and renewable energy initiatives—consistently supporting programs valued from $50M to over $2B.

We are registered in SAM.gov and positioned as a small disadvantaged business in a rural, underserved census tract—eligible for SBA mentor-protégé and federal set-aside programs.
